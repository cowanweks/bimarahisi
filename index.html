About Bima Rahisi

Bima Rahisi: ‘Easy Insurance’ –Kenyan focused, Mobile based, Insurance. All our products are ordered, fulfilled and paid for via the Mobile phone. Fast, Easy, Affordable and Trustworthy.

Our Products

Travel Insurance, Motor Insurance, Health Insurance & Micro Insurance (Hospital cash, Educate Protect, Internal Travel and Student Insurance)

Motor Insurance

Car insurance ensures that you do not suffer a setback /break the bank financially in case of an accident. But remember, it is not a one-size-fits-all requirement.

Travel Insurance

Kenya’s award-winning travel Insurance provider. We provide Annual, Single Trip, Local trips and Backpacker travel insurance to suit different trips abroad, Visa requirements, budgets and needs.

Hospital cash

Enables one to pay for hospitalization if one or one’s family are hospitalised from falling sick or after an accident. Coverage is for daily hospital expenses.

Educate Protect

We take the worry out of education costs in the event of something unfortunate happening

Personal Accident

The Personal Accident cover provides a financial back up in the event of injuries or death resulting from an accident.

Group Personal Accident

We work with employers/institutions to get the best cover for benefit of employees or for the benefit of members.

Agricultural Insurance Cover

We provide cover for dairy cattle, beef cattle, poultry, pigs, sheep and goats. We also provide cover for commercial crops such as; wheat, maize, barley, rice, tea crop, coffee, sugarcane, tobacco, all horticultural crops, floriculture and tree crops
